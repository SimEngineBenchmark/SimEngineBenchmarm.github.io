Postdoctoral Fellow                                                                                                                                               June, 2014 – May 2015

Linköping University and Volvo Car Corporation, Sweden

  4. •In-house designed algorithms for alarm generations, residuals monitoring as well as Fault Isolation (FI)

  5. •Simulation and FI of system with intermittent residuals
